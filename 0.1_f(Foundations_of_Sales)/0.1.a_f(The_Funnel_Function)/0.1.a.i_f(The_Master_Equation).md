1.1. The Final Probability of Awareness

The probability of conversion to awareness $P(\text{Awareness})$ is modeled as the Sigmoid function $\sigma(\cdot)$ of the instantaneous activation $\mathcal{A}$, emphasizing that awareness is a continuous threshold-crossing event, not a binary state.

$$P(\text{Awareness} \mid \mathcal{A}) = \sigma(\alpha \cdot \mathcal{A} - \beta) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(\alpha \cdot \mathcal{A} - \beta)}}$$

Where $\alpha$ is the sensitivity slope and $\beta$ is the fixed offset threshold.

Part 2: The Calculus of Perspective (Sender vs. Receiver)

The foresight of the equation is its applicability based on the perspective of the user (Receiver) or the operator (Sender). This creates a dual-layer calculus: the Sender engages in Optimization, while the Receiver’s experience is defined by Stochasticity.

2.1. Sender’s Calculus (The Maximization Problem)

The Sender controls the numerator ($\mathbf{S}, \mathbf{R}, \mathbf{\Pi}$) and seeks to maximize the Marginal Awareness Return (MAR) for every unit of cost $\lambda_c$.

The Marginal Awareness Return for a generic controllable factor $X \in \{\mathbf{S}, \mathbf{R}, \mathbf{\Pi}\}$ is:

$$\text{MAR}_X = \frac{\partial P(\text{Awareness})}{\partial \lambda_c} = \frac{\partial P}{\partial \mathcal{A}} \cdot \frac{\partial \mathcal{A}}{\partial X} \cdot \frac{\partial X}{\partial \lambda_c}$$

The optimal strategy involves satisfying the budget constraint $B$ via Lagrangian Optimization, ensuring the marginal return across all investment channels is equal to the Lagrangian multiplier $\lambda_{\text{budget}}$:

$$\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial \lambda_c} = \frac{\partial \mathbb{E}[I_{u,b,T}]}{\partial \lambda_c} - \lambda_{\text{budget}} = 0$$

2.2. Receiver’s Calculus (The Stochastic Experience)

The Receiver experiences the environment as a stochastic process where $\mathbf{N}$ and $\mathbf{L}$ are random variables. Awareness is defined by the probability density function $f(\mathcal{A} \mid \mathbf{L}, \mathbf{N})$.

The Integrated Activation Trace ($I$) is the decaying cumulative memory trace over time $T$, serving as the primary predictor of purchase behavior:

$$I_{u,b,T} = \sum_{t=0}^{T} \gamma^{T-t} \cdot \mathcal{A}_{u,m,t}(e_t)$$

This trace is governed by the differential equation, where $\lambda_{decay}$ is the memory decay rate:

$$\frac{dI_{u,b,t}}{dt} = - \lambda_{decay} I_{u,b,t} + \beta \cdot \mathcal{A}_{u,m,t}(e)$$

Part 3: The Calculus of Time (Foresight vs. Hindsight)

3.1. Prospective Application: Foresight (Prediction & Planning)

Foresight uses the calculus to predict the Integrated Activation Trace $\mathbb{E}[I]$ and allocate resources $\{x_{m,t}\}$ to maximize this value over the campaign horizon $T$.

The Denominator Resilience Maximization: Foresight is defined by the goal of maximizing the buffer ratio to ensure survival in predictable high-noise environments:

$$\max_{\lambda_{\pi}} \; \frac{\mathbf{\Pi}}{\mathbb{E}[\mathbf{N} + \mathbf{L} + \mathbf{\Theta}]}$$

The planning problem is solved by replacing the instantaneous terms with their expected values, $\mathbb{E}[\mathbf{N}]$ and $\mathbb{E}[\mathbf{L}]$, derived from time-series forecasting and contextual models.

3.2. Retrospective Application: Hindsight (Attribution & Audit)

Hindsight uses the calculus to audit past performance by isolating the Marginal Revenue Contribution (MRC) of each investment, decomposing the final purchase probability via the chain rule.

$$\text{MRC}_{\lambda_e} = \frac{\partial P(\text{Purchase})}{\partial \lambda_e} = \frac{\partial P}{\partial I} \cdot \frac{\partial I}{\partial \mathcal{A}} \cdot \frac{\partial \mathcal{A}}{\partial \lambda_e}$$

Counterfactual Attribution: The intrinsic performance of the creative ($\mathbf{S}, \mathbf{R}, \mathbf{\Pi}$) is isolated from environmental luck ($\mathbf{N}, \mathbf{L}$) using the Shapley Value on the numerator terms:

$$\text{Attribution}_{X} = \text{ShapleyValue}(X \mid Y, Z)$$


Where $X, Y, Z$ are the three numerator factors. This assigns credit based on a factor’s marginal contribution across all possible environments.
